To configure this application, you need a URL for your Primo instance. Copy the following URL and paste into the line below. It should look like the following:
https://[institution]-primo.hosted.exlibrisgroup.com/primo-explore/search?tab=default_tab&search_scope=[SCOPE]&vid=[VIEW]

Replace the parts in the [ ], including the brackets themselves, with the approprate values from your Primo instance:

[institution] = The part of your Primo instance url unique to your institution between the https:// and the '-primo'.

[SCOPE] = This is your consortial search scope.

[VIEW] = The View ID of your institution.
