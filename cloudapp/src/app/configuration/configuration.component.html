To configure this application, you need a URL for your Primo or Primo VE instance. Copy one of the following URLs, depending on which version of Primo you are using, and paste into the line below.

Non-PrimoVE users:
https://[institution].exlibrisgroup.com/primo-explore/search?tab=[TAB]&search_scope=[SCOPE]&vid=[VIEW]

Primo VE users:
https://[institution].exlibrisgroup.com/discovery/search?tab=[TAB]&search_scope=[SCOPE]&vid=[VIEW]

Replace the parts in the [ ], including the brackets themselves, with the approprate values from your Primo instance:

[institution] = The part of your Primo instance url unique to your institution between the https:// and the 'exlibrisgroup'.

[TAB] = The tab you are using for your search. If you don't have a specific tab, use 'default_tab'.

[SCOPE] = This is your consortial search scope.

[VIEW] = The View ID of your institution.
